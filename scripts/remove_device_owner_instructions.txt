How to Remove Device Owner Provisioning from BioScan Kiosk:

1. Open BioScan Kiosk on the phone.
2. Multi-tap the top-right Admin trigger button and enter the Administrator PIN (default: 123456).
3. Navigate to the "Settings & Backup" tab.
4. Tap "Exit LockTask Mode".
5. Connect phone to PC via USB debugging and execute:
   adb shell dpm remove-active-admin com.aistudio.bioscankiosk.app/com.example.bioscan.core.kiosk.BioScanDeviceAdminReceiver

Note: On some Android versions, removing Device Owner requires a factory reset if prohibited by policy.
